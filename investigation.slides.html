Ford GoBike 2008 Investigation¶

Ford GoBike is a bike-sharing system covering 5 cities. In this project I will be descoviring the greater San Francisco Bay area.
Data analysis had done on year 2008. The data is provided by www.fordgobike.com and it has 1863721 observations with 16 variables. The reason I chosed this data-set becuase it provide enough observations and variables that can be analysed.
For more exploration I have downloaded weather data-set by National Centers for Environmental Information.

conclusion¶

With 331 stations in San Francicso and over 1.86 million trips has been taken on 2008.The analysis shows that the bike share system is more active on business days than weekends. In terms of months there is a spike from May to Oct. We have seen some relation with weather Temprature. The analysis shows that Female take longer ride duration than male.
